FAQ

Quick answers about orders, shipping and care.

How do I order?

Add items to cart and click Checkout. The site will open WhatsApp with your order summary already written.

Do you deliver?

Yes. 2–5 business days (Lagos) • 3–10 business days (Nationwide) (timing may vary by location).

What is your return policy?

Because our pieces are handmade, we accept returns only for items received damaged. Contact us within 24 hours of delivery with photos.

How do I care for sculptural candles?

Keep away from direct heat/sunlight. Trim wick to 5mm. Place candles on a heat-safe tray.

Can I request custom scents/colors?

Yes. Tap “Custom Orders” or message us on WhatsApp with your idea.
